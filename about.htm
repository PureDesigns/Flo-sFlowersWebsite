About

Flo's Flowers is a locally owned business based out of Spring, Texas.
Created in 2019, we focus on providing you with beautiful arrangments and assortments with a guarantee of the best quality flowers. Flo's Flowers strives to be honest in all our dealings and to portray loyalty to our customers.

How to order

Choose your flowers or arrangments then call us to place the order. It's that simple. Within 24 hours your order will be ready to pickup in store.
